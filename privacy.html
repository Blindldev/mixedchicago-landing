Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 15th, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Sign up for our waitlist by providing your name and email address
  • Contact us through our website or email
  • Participate in surveys or other activities we may offer

The personal information we collect includes:

  • Name: First and last name for personalization and identification
  • Email Address: For communication about our services and events
  • Consent Records: Your agreement to receive communications from us
  • Communication Preferences: Your opt-in choices for different types of communications

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as social security numbers, financial information, or health data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following business purposes:

  • Service Communications: Send you updates about Mixed Mediums and our upcoming opening
  • Marketing Communications: Notify you about creative opportunities, workshops, and events at our space (with your consent)
  • Customer Service: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Business Operations: Improve our website and services, analyze usage patterns
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Personalization: Customize communications using your name for a better experience

We will only use your personal information for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as otherwise disclosed to you at the time of collection.

Email Communications (CAN-SPAM Compliant)

By providing your email address and checking the consent box, you expressly consent to receive commercial emails from us about:

  • Updates about Mixed Mediums' opening and launch
  • Information about creative workshops and events
  • Community announcements and opportunities
  • Special offers and promotions
  • Newsletters and educational content

Your Rights Under CAN-SPAM Act:

  • Every email we send will include a clear and conspicuous "unsubscribe" link
  • You can opt out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly
  • We will honor your unsubscribe request within 10 business days
  • We will not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties
  • All emails will clearly identify Mixed Mediums as the sender

Contact for Unsubscribe: You can also unsubscribe by emailing us at MixedChicago@gmail.com with "UNSUBSCRIBE" in the subject line.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except:

  • To trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure data storage and processing systems
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Staff training on data protection practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Active Subscribers: We retain your information while you remain subscribed to our communications
  • Unsubscribed Users: We may retain minimal information for legal compliance and to prevent re-subscription
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain information longer if required by applicable laws
  • Business Purposes: We retain information as needed for legitimate business operations

When we no longer need your personal information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Opt out of email communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time

California Residents (CCPA Rights)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to know whether we sell or disclose personal information
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price, even if you exercise your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at MixedChicago@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
